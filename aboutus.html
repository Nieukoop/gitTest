About us

Let me introduce myself, my name is Debbie Isaac and I am the proprietor of DSI Debt Recovery. I have worked as a Debt Collector/Credit Controller for over 12 years and have gained valuable experience dealing with bad debtors.

We are a local West Australian business who offer a personal and professional service. We understand the devastating effect unpaid debt can have on a business and we do everything possible to achieve the best results for our clients. We are proud to say that the majority of our business is through word of mouth.

DSI Debt Recovery offers a quality service tailored to individual business needs, and we understand the importance of communicating the recovery process with our clients.

Ultimately our aim at DSI Debt Recovery is to recover debt quickly with minimal fuss to our clients. This in turn will help inject money back into their business and will also allow them the time to focus on other important business matters.
